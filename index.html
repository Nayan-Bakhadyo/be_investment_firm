Our Services

Investment Management

Our investment management services are designed to align with your individual financial goals and risk tolerance. We follow a rigorous process that includes:

  • Investment Strategy: Funds are invested according to approved investment strategies.
  • Risk Management: We prioritize risk mitigation and diversification in all investment decisions.
  • Performance Tracking: Fund performance is regularly monitored to ensure alignment with investment objectives.

Portfolio Diversification

We employ diversification strategies across various asset classes to minimize risk and enhance potential returns.

Risk Management

B.E Investments takes a proactive approach to risk management. Our process includes:

  • Risk Assessment: Identifying and assessing potential risks (market, credit, liquidity, operational).
  • Risk Mitigation: Implementing strategies to mitigate identified risks.
  • Portfolio Monitoring: Regularly monitoring and rebalancing the fund's portfolio to maintain an appropriate risk-return profile.
  • Stress Testing: Conducting stress tests and scenario analysis to evaluate the fund's performance under various market conditions.

Reporting and Performance Evaluation

B.E Investments is committed to providing our investors with transparent and regular reports on fund performance:

  • Transaction Confirmation: Investors receive immediate confirmation of all investment transactions.
  • Reporting: We provide monthly/quarterly reports summarizing fund performance, returns, and holdings. Investors also receive fund statements and reports detailing their portfolio's performance and balance.
  • Performance Evaluation: Fund performance is compared against relevant benchmarks.

STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

Account Opening

To open an account with B.E Investments:

  • Submit a completed Account Opening Form (AOF) along with Know Your Customer (KYC) documents.
  • A unique Account Number will be assigned to each investor.
  • Investor details are maintained confidentially and securely.

AOP and KYC forms will be sent to your email with bank details. After verification of opening you can start transferring funds on monthly or weekly basis.

Withdrawal Requests

To submit a withdrawal request:

  • Investors must submit a withdrawal request specifying the amount or units to redeem.
  • Standard withdrawal requests require 30 days' prior notice. Emergency withdrawal requests may be considered on a case-by-case basis.
  • Redemptions are processed based on the fund's net asset value (NAV) on the applicable valuation date.
  • Funds are transferred to the investor's designated bank account upon successful processing.
  • A confirmation of redemption, including details of the amount withdrawn and remaining units, is sent to the investor.

Fund Reporting

We provide transparent and regular reports to investors on the fund's performance.

  • Quarterly/Annual reports: Detailed reports are generated, including share value, returns, sector-wise allocation, and portfolio changes.
  • Fund performance is compared against relevant benchmarks.
  • Reports are sent to investors along with any changes to the fund's strategy, risks, or fees.

Investor Communication

We maintain clear and consistent communication with investors regarding their investments.

  • Investors are informed about important updates via email, SMS, or postal mail.
  • Changes in fund policies, strategies, or fees
  • Fund performance and share value updates
  • Regulatory changes that may affect the fund
